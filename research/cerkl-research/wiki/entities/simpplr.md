## Overview

Modern-intranet platform vendor; recognized as Forrester Wave Leader (Intranet Platforms Q2 2026 — see [[forrester-wave-intranet-platforms-q2-2026]]) and Gartner Magic Quadrant Leader (3rd year, 4.6/5 G2).
