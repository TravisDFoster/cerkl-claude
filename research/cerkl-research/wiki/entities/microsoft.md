# Microsoft

**Type:** Enterprise software and cloud vendor  |  **Founded:** 1975  |  **Website:** https://www.microsoft.com

## Overview

Microsoft is the parent company of [[linkedin]] (acquired 2016) and the operator of the Microsoft Viva employee-experience platform. In 2026 it added a new IC-relevant data point: the **Rule of 70 voluntary retirement program** (announced 2026-04-23) — the highest-scale "no-memo" approach in the 2026 layoff-comms subgenre.

## Key Ideas / Contributions

- **Rule of 70 voluntary buyout (2026):** ~8,750 US employees eligible (~7% of US workforce); notifications 2026-05-07; 30-day decision window closing 2026-06-06. AI/Copilot teams explicitly exempted from the offer and the prior hiring freeze.
- **Absence-of-memo as comms strategy** — Microsoft chose not to publish a layoff memo; the entire framing is "voluntary retirement" with no public CEO-level acknowledgment. Effective for corporate brand, criticized by labor commentators as the "voluntary" euphemism for pushed-out.
- Owner of [[linkedin]] (acquired 2016) and operator of Microsoft Viva / [[glint]].
