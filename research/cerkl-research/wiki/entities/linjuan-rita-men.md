# Linjuan Rita Men

**Type:** Academic researcher (person)  |  **Role:** Professor of Public Relations, University of Florida, College of Journalism and Communications

## Overview

UF PR scholar; author of *"Artificial intelligence for internal communication: Strategies, challenges, and implications"* (Public Relations Review; n=20 senior comms professionals via in-depth interviews) — the qualitative companion to [[peter-cardon]] & [[anthony-coman]]'s 2025 quantitative study. Voices the [[co-piloting]] prescription: *"Co-piloting is the best approach. Enhance the efficiency and efficacy of the work, but always have a human in the center."*

## Key Ideas / Contributions

- **Co-piloting** as the academic prescription for AI in IC — human in the center, AI for polish.
- **The killer practitioner-side quote** (from her interview set): *"If my manager has to use AI to write a message like this, why are they my manager at all?"* — academically-sourced, citation-grade.
- Senior comms practitioners' actual sentiment captured qualitatively (n=20) — complements Cardon & Coman's quantitative work.
