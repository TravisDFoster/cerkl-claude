# HR Reporter (Canadian HR Reporter)

**Type:** Trade publication (HR / People Ops / L&D audience, Canadian-anchored)  |  **Website:** https://www.hrreporter.com  |  **Parent:** Key Media / Thomson Reuters affiliated

## Overview

Canadian HR-focused trade publication covering HR strategy, employment law, automation, and AI for HR practitioners. Audience skews HR/L&D, not pure IC — Canadian Bill C-27 / AIDA regulatory subtext is common. Distinct from [[ragan-communications]] / [[pr-daily]] (IC-pure trade press) and treats internal communications as one of several HR sub-disciplines.

## Key Ideas / Contributions

- **The AI-skeptic frame for HR audiences:** explicit research-backed counter to AI-maximalist narratives; anchored in academic sources rather than vendor or consulting voices.
- **Audience caveat:** HR Reporter pieces land less heavily with senior IC directors at Fortune 500s; load-bearing for People Ops / HR generalists who also run internal comms.
