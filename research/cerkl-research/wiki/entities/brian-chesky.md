# Brian Chesky

**Type:** Person  |  **Role:** Co-founder and CEO, [[airbnb]]

## Overview

Brian Chesky is the co-founder and CEO of [[airbnb]] and the author of the 2020-05-05 layoff memo that has anchored "humane layoff comms" reference frameworks for six years. The memo is the gold standard ICs cite when grading 2026 AI-restructure memos — and the structural reason GitLab's "Act 2" memo gets compared favorably.

## Key Ideas / Contributions

- **The 2020-05-05 Airbnb layoff memo:** 3,374 words, led with the financial "why" (COVID-driven revenue collapse), named the specific teams affected, then detailed concrete severance: 14 weeks base + tenure bonus, 12 months COBRA in US, year-end health coverage globally, equity cliff waiver, laptop keep, alumni placement directory.
- The structural innovation: pairing **transparency** with **material support** — not language alone. The reason the memo aged well is that the support was real, not rhetorical.
