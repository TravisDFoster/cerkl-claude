## Overview

Bain is one of the third-leg consulting-firm voices on 2026 AI transformation alongside [[mckinsey]] and [[bcg]]. Its angle is operating-model redesign — roles as supervisors of agentic systems — rather than the talent-development or behavioral-design frames its peers anchor on.

## Key Ideas / Contributions

- Operating-model frame: roles as supervisors of agentic systems; parallel workflow + workforce modernization.
