# Perceptyx

**Type:** Employee-listening / EX research vendor  |  **Website:** https://www.perceptyx.com/

## Overview

Perceptyx is one of the larger employee-listening platforms — surveys + people analytics + continuous-listening tooling. Its public EX-trends research positions it as a counter-voice to [[qualtrics]] in the 2026 change-engagement debate: where Qualtrics says more change = more engaged, Perceptyx's ~20M-response dataset emphasizes that *"change fatigue doesn't discriminate between positive and negative change"* and that perceptions of change handling have declined two consecutive years.

## Key Ideas / Contributions

- 2026 EX Trends Report: "change management effectiveness" and "confidence in senior leadership" **displaced "belonging"** as the top engagement drivers in 2025 — names leadership credibility, not change exposure, as the lever.
- Articulates [[change-fatigue]] as universal regardless of change valence (positive or negative).
- Operates the **20M-response dataset** that gives it the credibility to push back on Qualtrics' n=33,831 finding.
