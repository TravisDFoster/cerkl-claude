# Qualtrics EmployeeXM

**Type:** Experience-management platform vendor (employee-experience surface: EmployeeXM)  |  **Website:** https://www.qualtrics.com/employee-experience/

## Overview

Qualtrics is the heavyweight comparator in the employee-listening / EX space — strongest statistical depth, predictive modeling, and benchmarking, paired with 8–12 week professional-services rollouts. Pitched for orgs that need rigorous research-grade analysis over fast, continuous insight.

## Key Ideas / Contributions

- 2026 Global Employee Experience Trends Report (n=33,831, 24 countries; fieldwork Sept–Oct 2025): the boldest 2026 EX-research claim — *"those experiencing more change today are generally more engaged."* Conditional on change type and listening cadence.
- Operationalizes the moderator as **listening frequency** (87% engagement at high-listening orgs vs 44% at low) rather than the broader comms-quality frame.
- [[benjamin-granger]] (Chief Workplace Psychologist) is the public spokesperson and coiner of the *"Goldilocks zone"* framing.
