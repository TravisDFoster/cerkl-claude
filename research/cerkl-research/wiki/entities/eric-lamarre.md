# Eric Lamarre

**Type:** Person  |  **Role:** Senior Partner, [[mckinsey]] (McKinsey Digital practice)  |  **Website:** https://www.mckinsey.com/our-people/eric-lamarre

## Overview

Eric Lamarre is a Senior Partner in McKinsey's Digital practice and the lead author of the *Rewired* book series, the firm's flagship playbook on enterprise digital and AI transformation. The 2nd edition (2026) shifts the subtitle from "Outcompeting in the Age of Digital and AI" to "How Leading Companies Win with Technology and AI" and adds the talent-transformation thesis as the operational center.

## Key Ideas / Contributions

- Lead author of *Rewired* (1st ed. 2023 with [[kate-smaje]] and Rodney Zemmel; 2nd ed. 2026 with [[kate-smaje]], [[rob-levin]], plus book co-authors Alex Singla and Alexander Sukharevsky).
- Articulator of the talent-transformation thesis: *"At the heart of every digital transformation is a talent transformation."* ([[talent-transformation-thesis]])
- Co-author of the 2026-05-04 *Rewired 2.0: The Talent Plan* Organization Blog post.
