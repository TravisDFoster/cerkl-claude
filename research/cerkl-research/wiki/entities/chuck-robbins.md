# Chuck Robbins

**Type:** Person  |  **Role:** Chair and CEO, [[cisco]]

## Overview

Chuck Robbins is the Chair and CEO of [[cisco]] and the author of the 2026-05-13 *"Our Path Forward"* memo announcing ~4,000 cuts alongside a record $15.8B Q3 FY26 quarter. The memo is the 2026 anchor for the [[strong-quarter-layoff]] pattern — cutting from a position of financial strength, leading with revenue, then pivoting to reinvestment in silicon/optics/security/AI.

## Key Ideas / Contributions

- Author of the 2026-05-13 *"Our Path Forward"* memo. Opening line led with record Q3 FY26 revenue ($15.8B, +12% YoY) before pivoting to the cuts.
- Memo line that IC trade press praised: *"focus, urgency, and the discipline to continuously shift investment."*
- Memo line on rationale: *"While we are reducing roles in some areas, we are making clear, strategic investments – particularly in silicon, optics, security, and in our employees' use of AI across the company."*
- Tangible offer to affected: pro-rated FY26 bonus, ~75% placement-rate services, **one free year of Cisco U** (all AI/Security/Networking certs).
