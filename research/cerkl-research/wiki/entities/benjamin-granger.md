# Benjamin Granger

**Type:** Person  |  **Role:** Chief Workplace Psychologist, [[qualtrics]]

## Overview

Dr. Benjamin Granger is the senior public face of Qualtrics' EX research and is the named spokesperson for the 2026 Global Employee Experience Trends Report. His public framing of the *"Goldilocks zone of change"* anchors the most-cited 2026 EX research claim that employees experiencing more change are *more* engaged — provided the change is investment-coded and listening cadence is steady.

## Key Ideas / Contributions

- Coined and publicly anchored the [[goldilocks-zone-of-change]] frame: *"The challenge for 2026 is to find the sweet spot of enough pressure to make work engaging and fulfilling, and enough change to keep things fresh and interesting, but not so much that they stress, burn out or alienate your people."*
- Operationalizes the moderator of the change-engagement relationship as **listening frequency** (87% engagement at high-listening orgs vs 44% at low) rather than the broader comms-quality frame.
- Frames the listening prescription as *"honest, two-way conversations that keep employees connected with your organization despite the change."*
