# Rob Levin

**Type:** Person  |  **Role:** Senior Partner, [[mckinsey]]

## Overview

Rob (Robert) Levin is a Senior Partner at McKinsey who joined the *Rewired* 2nd-edition author lineup in 2026 (the 1st edition was Lamarre / Smaje / Zemmel). His addition to the book parallels the strategic shift from generic digital transformation to AI-and-agentic workforce design — Levin is positioned as a primary voice on the 70-30 talent flips and the human-agentic collaboration material.
