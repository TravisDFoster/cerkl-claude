# Meta

**Type:** Social-media and AI platform vendor (Facebook, Instagram, WhatsApp, Threads, Reality Labs)  |  **Founded:** 2004 (as Facebook)  |  **Website:** https://about.meta.com

## Overview

Meta is the operator of Facebook, Instagram, WhatsApp, Threads, and Reality Labs. In 2026 it joined the AI-restructure layoff-memo subgenre with Mark Zuckerberg's 2026-05-20 *"success isn't a given"* memo — widely read as self-justification to remaining staff and not empathy for departing employees. Blind sentiment data tracked the immediate fallout: culture rating dropped **39%** and overall rating **25%** from Q2 2024 peak.

## Key Ideas / Contributions

- **Zuckerberg 2026-05-20 memo:** *"Success isn't a given"* + *"AI is the most consequential technology of our lifetimes."* Framed by press as a warning to *remaining* staff, not as empathy for departing ones.
- Coverage included an "8,000-cut restructure" frame (see also the dual-memo Zuckerberg-plus-CPO pattern surfaced in 2026-05-22 reporting).
- Adjacent prior IC controversy: Bosworth's no-opt-out activity-tracking memo (2026-05-13) drew internal backlash on privacy grounds.
