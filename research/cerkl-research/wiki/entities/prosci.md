# Prosci

**Type:** Change-management research and consulting firm  |  **Website:** https://www.prosci.com/

## Overview

Prosci is the canonical reference body for organizational change management methodology, best known for the ADKAR framework (Awareness, Desire, Knowledge, Ability, Reinforcement). Its change-saturation research is the threshold-model counterweight to the 2026 [[qualtrics]] finding — Prosci argues engagement rises with change only up to a cumulative-events threshold, then inverts sharply.

## Key Ideas / Contributions

- **ADKAR framework** — the dominant practitioner change-management methodology (Awareness, Desire, Knowledge, Ability, Reinforcement).
- **Change saturation research:** 73% of change-affected employees report moderate-to-high stress; engagement rises with change only below the saturation threshold, then inverts. Forces IC to track *cumulative concurrent change-events*, not just comms cadence.
