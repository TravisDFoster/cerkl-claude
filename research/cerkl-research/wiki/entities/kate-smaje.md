**Type:** Person  |  **Role:** Senior Partner, [[mckinsey]] (McKinsey Digital practice)  |  **Website:** https://www.mckinsey.com/our-people/kate-smaje

## Overview

Kate Smaje is a Senior Partner in McKinsey's Digital practice and co-author of the *Rewired* book series (1st and 2nd editions). Her contribution centers on the operating-model and capability-building threads of McKinsey's digital and AI transformation playbook.

## Key Ideas / Contributions

- Co-author of *Rewired* (1st ed. 2023, 2nd ed. 2026) with [[eric-lamarre]] and [[rob-levin]] (2nd ed.).
- Co-author of the 2026-05-04 *Rewired 2.0: The Talent Plan* Organization Blog post.
- Public spokesperson on the McKinsey Live webinar series ("Rewired to win: Reimagining the enterprise with tech and AI").
