# Deloitte

**Type:** Professional services and consulting firm  |  **Founded:** 1845  |  **Website:** https://www.deloitte.com

## Overview

Deloitte is one of the dominant 2026 publishers of large-sample workforce research, primarily through the annual *Global Human Capital Trends* report. Its 2026 edition ("Human Advantage") is the de-facto softer, EX-led counter-narrative to [[mckinsey]]'s *Rewired 2.0* — adaptability as the binding constraint rather than program design or talent-model mechanics.

## Key Ideas / Contributions

- **2026 Global Human Capital Trends ("Human Advantage")** — 65% of organizations say culture must shift significantly because of AI; 85% say adaptability is critical, only 7% leading on it; only 6% making progress on human-AI work design.
- **Adaptability as the underweighted lever** — different from McKinsey's "talent and comms infrastructure" framing; treats comms as a sub-component of organizational adaptability.
- **EX-centric framing** — human-centric approaches to AI are *"significantly more likely to exceed ROI expectations than those focused primarily on technology deployment."*
