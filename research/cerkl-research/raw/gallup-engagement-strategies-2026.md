## Key Statistics (verbatim from source)

- "organizations with highly engaged teams see 23% higher profitability, up to 18% higher productivity and 51% less turnover"
- "Disengaged employees cost the U.S. an estimated $2 trillion in lost productivity"
- "less than one in three employees feel strongly connected to their company's mission"
- "less than half of employees (47%) strongly agree they know what is expected of them at work"
- "Only 26% say their organization has communicated a clear plan for integrating AI"
- "employees whose managers actively support AI use are 2.1 times as likely to use it a few times a week or more"
- "less than half of U.S. employees participate in job-related training"
- "59% of CHROs said development is one of the elements of the employee experience their organization struggles with most, up 16 percentage points from the prior year"
- "Managers account for 70% of the variance in team-level engagement"

## Three Engagement Strategies for 2026

The guide addresses three engagement challenges centered on:

1. **Clarity** — Ensuring employees understand expectations and organizational mission
2. **Connection** — Building emotional attachment to workplace and company purpose
3. **Development** — Investing in employee learning and growth opportunities

## Research Basis

Based on Gallup's broader research portfolio: "Gallup has surveyed over 3 million employees and coached more than 100,000 teams."
