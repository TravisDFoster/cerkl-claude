# IC Trends — 2026-05-18

**Today's headline takeaway:** Two themes broke through since Friday — competitors are moving from post-send measurement to **pre-send AI prediction** (ContactMonkey's "Audience Preview"), and practitioners are shifting the conversation from message *volume* to message *format* (Cisco/Ragan pushing short-form video, Yale/Roku newsletter teardowns). On the news side, the week's flashpoint is **Microsoft shutting down internal employee-voice channels** — circulating as the cautionary tale every IC lead is being asked about.
