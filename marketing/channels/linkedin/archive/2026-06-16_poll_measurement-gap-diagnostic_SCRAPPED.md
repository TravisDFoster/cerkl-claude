## Caption

The Insights / Measurement webinar on June 25 opens with a diagnostic. Before Rachel walks through the May 2026 Insights redesign, attendees self-sort by where their measurement work is breaking down.

Most IC teams have one of four gaps eating the week. Open rate as the only number leadership sees, so it gets treated as the proof point even when it isn't one. Reports that lump every employee together, so the team can't say what landed with the frontline vs the corporate office. Channel-by-channel dashboards that don't talk to each other, so a Teams post and an email blast each take credit for the same employee. Or no through-line from a send to a behavior, so when a CHRO asks "did the policy update land," the honest answer is "we sent it."

All four are real problems. Most weeks one of them is the one slowing the team down.

Which gap is yours this quarter? Webinar registration in the first comment.
