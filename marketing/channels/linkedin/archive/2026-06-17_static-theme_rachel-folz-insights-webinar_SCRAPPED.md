## Caption

Most IC teams can tell you the open rate. They can't tell you who acted on the message.

When a CHRO asks "did the new benefits policy land with the frontline," the answer most teams have to give is "we sent it, and 38% opened." That isn't the question.

Open rate is the easiest number to produce and the easiest to defend, so it became the default report. Useful, but not enough to hold the function's ground when the budget conversation comes around.

The measurement layer IC teams need ties a send to a segment, a segment to an outcome, and an outcome to a number the CHRO can put in a board update. Per-audience, per-channel, per-content. With the receipts the compliance team also asks for.

The May 2026 Insights redesign in Broadcast was rebuilt around that question. Rachel Folz, our Head of Product, is walking through it on June 25 at 12 PM ET.

Bring the dashboard your CHRO last asked for. She'll show you how to build it.

45 minutes, free, no card. Link in comments.
