## Caption

The IC identity crisis of 2026: AI moved into the tactical work IC used to own (the drafting, the formatting, the segmentation). Leadership has not, yet, moved IC into the conversations where the work IC should now own gets decided.

That gap is what "be more strategic" points at without saying.

The Cerkl blog from Monday names the five decisions IC managers and directors should be in the room for in 2026. The four conversations to start having now to get pulled into those rooms. And the executive vocabulary to use once you are there.

For practitioners caught between a tactical layer that is shrinking and a strategic layer that is still gatekept, the piece is a working playbook. The language. The metrics. The specific moves.
