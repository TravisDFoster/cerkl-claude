## Caption

"Be more strategic" is the most useless advice an IC manager gets from their CHRO. It names the thing without telling you how.

There are five decisions IC should be in the room for in 2026: AI rollout and policy, change communications for M&A or restructuring, crisis comms, the employee value proposition, and cross-functional measurement of engagement and retention signals. Each one has a version where IC is consulted late, and a version where IC is the architect.

Four specific conversations move you from the first version to the second. The new blog walks through the language to use, the metrics to bring, and the executive-vocabulary upgrade that gets IC pulled into the strategy meeting earlier.

For IC managers and directors caught between AI taking the tactical work and not being in the leadership conversation yet, this is a practitioner's playbook, not a pep talk.
